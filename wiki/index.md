## Quick Start — Material Progression

1. **Adamantite** — Starter endgame gear. Craft your first armor set.
2. **Mithril** — Frozen Dungeon drops. Mana regeneration on armor.
3. **Onyxium** — Swamp Dungeon & Golem Void drops. HP regen on armor.
4. **Prisma** — Endgame pinnacle. Unique signature attacks on every weapon.
